About Our School

Spring Garden Secondary School is dedicated to quality education, character development, and student success. The school serves approximately 400 students with a staff of 35 teachers, under the guidance of a principal and vice principal.

Principal’s Message

Welcome to Spring Garden Secondary School. We are committed to nurturing disciplined, respectful, and ambitious students through meaningful learning experiences, strong values, and modern opportunities in technology and innovation.

School Prayer

Heavenly Father, bless our school, our teachers, and our students. Guide us in wisdom, discipline, and respect so that we may learn, grow, and serve with excellence each day. Amen.

School Song

Spring Garden Secondary stands with pride,
Learning and leading side by side.
With discipline, courage, truth, and care,
We build bright futures everywhere.

School Rules

  • Students must be punctual to school and classes.
  • Proper uniform must be worn at all times.
  • Respect must be shown to teachers, peers, and school property.
  • Homework and assignments must be completed on time.
  • Students must maintain good conduct on and off the school compound.
